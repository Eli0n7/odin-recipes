instant coffee

Description

You're a coffee addict?You don't want to spend hundred of dollars in Starbucks?Instant coffee is the solution.It's not as good as a Starbucks, but it will do the trick.It's easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Filtered water
  • Instant coffee
  • Sugar

steps

  1. mix some water with sugar in a cup
  2. Boil water
  3. Pour the content of the sachet in the cup
  4. Pour the boiling water in the cup and stir
